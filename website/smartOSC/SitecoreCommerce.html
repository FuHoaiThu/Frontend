Sitecore Commerce B2B Accelerator

Ecommerce agencies want the best for their B2B clients. However, an effective wholesale system has distinct demands and requires unique capabilities when compared to B2C. The Sitecore Commerce B2B Accelerator is designed to completely enhance Sitecore Experience Commerce, tailoring it to wholesale processes and allowing it to:

  • Consolidate all B2B management processes

  • Simplify complex ordering functions

  • Improve customer experience

This brochure will outline the key benefits for both agencies and B2B clients and explain the outstanding features for storefront and backend including personalized catalog, quick order, merchandising panel and sales rep management.
